Hi, I am Bishal Kumar Jaiswal, a student at St. Xavier College in Maitighar, Kathmandu. My friends and family often describe my best quality as my empathy. They justify it by pointing to my consistent ability to understand and relate to the feelings and experiences of others. They've observed that I'm a good listener, always willing to offer a shoulder to lean on or a compassionate ear to those who need it. My loved ones appreciate my ability to put myself in their shoes and provide emotional support. I believe that empathy is a fundamental aspect of human connection and helps me build strong, meaningful relationships with the people in my life.
